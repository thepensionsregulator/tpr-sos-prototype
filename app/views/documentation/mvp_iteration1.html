Documentation

This documentation is for the research conducted between 9th - 12th April with internal users

We tested the end to end journey, allowing the users to upload statement of strategy, supporting documents and selecting if they are in deficit or not. Below is a list of pain points for users and a potential way forward to solve the issue.
