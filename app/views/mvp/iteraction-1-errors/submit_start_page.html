Important

This service is only for schemes with a valuation effective date on or after 22 September 2024.

Submit a scheme valuation

Use this service to submit valuation information for a defined benefit (DB) pension scheme.

We use the information you provide us to check that your scheme is appropriately funded and meets statutory funding requirements.

If your scheme valuation effective date is before 22 September 2024, and the scheme was in deficit on that date, use the Recovery plan service through Exchange (opens in new tab).

How to use this service

During the submission you will need to upload:

  • a copy of the statement of strategy spreadsheet, signed off by the chair of trustees
  • a copy of the acturial valuation report, signed off by scheme actuary

If your scheme is in deficit at the effective date of the valuation, you will also need to upload:

  • a copy of the recovery plan
  • a copy of schedule of contributions

Make sure you have all of these documents available as any information not submited will be lost.

Find out what you need to include on our Statement of strategy guidance.
