MVP 1 backlog

This page is a public record of our designs and it tells the story of what we’ve designed and why for MVP1. The updates published below describe the development of new features to address user needs or changes in the legislation, iterations of existing features, or findings from user research.

Prototype scope

Reflect a basic end-to-end journey for users to submit scheme valuation data.

Prototype value proposition

• Introduce Submit Scheme Valuation Data as new service to collect quality scheme data
• Improvements in existing services linked to valuation data (recovery plan)
• Reflect changes in the legislation
• Implement TPR new brand
• General customer experience improvements
