Submit your scheme valuation data

Use this service to submit valuation information for your defined benefit (DB) scheme. We use the information you provide us to check that your scheme is appropriately funded and meets your statutory funding objectives.

How to use this service

We will ask you to submit the following range of data and information:

  • Actuarial data
  • Covenant data
  • Funding and investment strategy
  • Actuarial valuation
  • Schedule of contribution

If your scheme does not meet the statutory funding objective, you must also submit a:

  • recovery plan

Download and use our templates

We have provided specific templates for each set of data. Download these templates and use them to provide the data.

The templates enable us to automatically check the data you have provided. Therefore you will not be able to upload data that does not sit within the templates.
