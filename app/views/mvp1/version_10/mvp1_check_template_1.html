Check the template you need for your scheme

Use this service to check what templates you must to use to submit your scheme valuation data.

How to use this service

You'll need to repond 5 questions:

  • what was the effective date of the most recent scheme funding (Part 3) valuation?
  • what is the relevant date for the scheme?
  • as at [relevant date], did the scheme have a 100 or more members?
  • was the scheme in deficit at the valuation effective date?
  • is the valuation submission Fast Track or Bespoke?

Your answers will determine the template you need to use, and we will publish the template file for you to download and start using.
