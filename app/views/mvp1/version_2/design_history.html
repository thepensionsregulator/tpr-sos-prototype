What has been changing from previous version?
Subject Description
Prototype scope The prototype introduces a end-to-end view of the process from login to send a new submission or manage an existing submission.
Value proposition This end-to-end view allows users to manage efficiently all the aspects of the pension schemes that they are managing including notifications related to the tasks assigned to users to manage the pension schemes.
Version updates Changes from Design team
The proposed UI design combines some of the new styleguide design patterns as well the GDS design system.
The new design introcuded navigation to make more easy to users to access and manage different funtionality and content across Exchange.
The new navigation introduces 'dashboard', 'notifications' and 'help and support' sections. This new sections need content and functionality to be defined by the product team and introduced in the prototype version 5.
