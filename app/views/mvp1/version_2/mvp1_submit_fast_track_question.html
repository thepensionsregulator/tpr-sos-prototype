Is the valuation submission fast track or bespoke?

Fast Track is a possible approach to DB funding to provide trustees with a simpler path to demonstrating compliance.
It represents our view of tolerated risk for a scheme and sets out a series of quantitative parameters that need to be met. If a scheme meets all the Fast Track parameters, we are unlikely to scrutinise the valuation submission further.
