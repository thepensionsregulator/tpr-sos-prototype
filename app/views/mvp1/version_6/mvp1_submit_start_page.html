Submit your scheme valuation data

Use this service to submit valuation information for your defined benefit (DB) scheme. We use the information you provide us to check that your scheme is appropriately funded and meets your statutory funding objectives.

How to use this service

You'll need to submit 2 separate PDF files:

  • signed statement of strategy
  • supporting document

The signed statement of strategy will contain the following details:

  • a trustee assessment
  • actuarial information
  • investment information
  • covenant information
  • the signed statement of strategy

The supporting document will contain the following details:

  • copy of the actuarial valuation

If the scheme is in deficit at the valuation effective date, you'll need to add the following details to the supporting document:

  • copy of the schedule of contributions
  • copy of the recovery plan

The signed statement of strategy document must be filled using one of our templates.
The templates will collect different information depending of the scheme details.
It is very important you choose, download, fill and upload later, the right template depending of your scheme details:
