This service is only for schemes with a valuation effective date on or after 22 September 2024.

Submit your scheme valuation reports

Use this service to submit valuation information for a Defined Benefit Scheme.

If your scheme valuation effective date is before 22 September 2024, and the scheme was in deficit on that date, use the Recovery plan service through Exchange.

We use this information you provide us to check that your scheme is appropriately funded and meets statutory funding requirements.

How to use this service

During the submission you will need to upload:

  • a copy of the statement of strategy, signed by the chair of trustees
  • a copy of the acturial valuation report, signed by scheme actuary

Schemes in deficit at the effective date of the valuation will also need to upload:

  • a copy of the recovery plan
  • a copy of schedule of contributions

You will need to have all of this information to hand as any information not submitted will be lost.
