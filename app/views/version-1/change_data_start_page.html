Change data for submitted scheme valuation 56789012

Use this service when TPR ask you to change data in a submitted scheme valuation for your defined benefit (DB) scheme.

How to use this service

You can change the following details when requested by TPR:

  • general information
  • actuarial information
  • investment information
  • covenant information
  • the copy of the actuarial valuation

The requested changes will be completed via web form (no need for re-send a new spreadsheet file). Any changes on the data will require trustee approval (no need for sign-off a new Statement of Strategy).

If your scheme is in deficit at the valuation effective date, we could ask you to send or update:

  • a copy of the schedule of contributions
  • a copy of the recovery plan

In some cases you could be asked to:

  • respond enquiries from TPR
  • attach additional documents when needed

Responding enquiries or attach additional documents can be completed using a different service.

What is next

Once we have received your changes, we will ask to your trustee to aporove the changes. The approved changes will be added to the signed-off statement of Strategy.

If your changes do not meet TPR's risk acceptance criteria, we will reject your strategy statement and ask you to submit a new scheme valuation.
