Submit scheme valuation data

Use this service to submit valuation information for your defined benefit (DB) scheme. We use the information you provide us to check that your scheme is appropriately funded and meets your statutory funding objectives.

How to use this service

You'll need to submit:

  • a trustee assessment
  • actuarial information
  • investment information
  • covenant information
  • a copy of the actuarial valuation
  • the signed statement of strategy

If your scheme is in deficit at the valuation effective date, you must also submit a:

  • copy of the schedule of contributions
  • copy of the recovery plan
